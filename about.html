About RHC

The RHC "gang" was founded in 1970. The first meeting of the hunt club was called at Art Reavie's farm on the Crookston Road outside of Tweed on the evening of April 18, 1970. There were 11 hunters present, and all expressed a strong interest in the proposed camp and club.
